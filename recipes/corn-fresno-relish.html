Corn Fresno Relish

Chef Lester Lepiten

Description

A nice relish to be enjoyed on a sandwich or as a standaline side

Ingredients

  • Corn
  • Fresno Chiles
  • Champagne Vinegar
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Remove corn from cob and blitz in a food processor until a majority of the kernels are broken while still having a chunky texture
  2. Mince seeded Fresno Peppers
  3. Combine in a mixing bowl with Champagne Vinegar
  4. Salt & Pepper to taste
