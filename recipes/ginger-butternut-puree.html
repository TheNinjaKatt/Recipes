Ginger Butternutt Puree

Chef Arvin Salazar

Description

A simple puree of roasted butternut squash with ginger.

Ingredients

  • Butternut Squash
  • Ground Ginger
  • Heavy Cream
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Peel, seed, dice, and roast the Butternut Squash.
  2. Combine with Ground Ginger and Heavy Cream in a food processor and spin until smooth.
  3. Salt & Pepper to taste.
